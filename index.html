Divya Kothandaraman

I am a Computer Science PhD candidate at the University of Maryland College Park, working with Prof. Dinesh Manocha and Prof. Ming Lin at the GAMMA Lab. My primary research focus is computer vision and deep learning. Previously, I was an undergraduate (five year dual degree) at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, where I obtained a bachelors degree in Electrical Engineering, and masters degree in Data Sciences.

My current research is in generative AI, with a special focus on image personalization and view synthesis. In the past, I have worked on video action recognition and domain adaptation.

ArXiv
arXiv / GitHub

Divya Kothandaraman, Tianyi Zhou, Ming Lin, Dinesh Manocha
Siggraph Asia 2023 (Conference Proceedings, Technical Communications)

A text-guided image to image diffusion model to generate aerial views from a single ground-view image.
